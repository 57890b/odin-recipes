Honey Garlic Chicken Skewers

Taste the Sweet and Savory Flavors with Honey Garlic Chicken Skewers: A Delicious Grilling Recipe!

Indulge in the irresistible blend of sweet honey and savory garlic with our succulent Honey Garlic Chicken Skewers.

List of ingredients to make Honey Garlic Chicken Skewers (5-6 Skewers)

Marinade

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and minced
  • 1 teaspoon minced ginger
  • 3 tablespoons dark soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons honey

Skewers

  • 2 chicken breasts, approximately 350g, chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped into large chunks
  • 1 onion, peeled and sliced into squares
  • 3 sticks of coriander/cilantro, roughly torn

How to make the skewers

  1. Combine all the marinade ingredients in a small bowl: 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, 2 cloves minced garlic, 1 teaspoon minced ginger, 3 tablespoons dark soy sauce, and 3 tablespoons honey.
  2. Place the chicken pieces in a bowl and pour three-quarters of the marinade over them (reserve the remaining marinade for basting during cooking). Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or up to a day.
  3. If using wooden skewers, soak them in water to prevent burning, or use metal skewers.
  4. Preheat your BBQ or griddle until very hot.
  5. Thread the chicken onto skewers, alternating with chunks of red pepper and onion. Discard any excess marinade from the bowl.
  6. Cook the skewers on the BBQ or griddle for 8-10 minutes, turning 3-4 times during cooking. Brush with the reserved marinade halfway through cooking. Ensure the chicken is fully cooked by slicing open the largest piece to check for any pinkness.
  7. Sprinkle the cooked skewers with torn coriander leaves before serving. Enjoy with salad and rice or potatoes, such as Greek Potatoes, if desired.
