Shakshuka

Easy Shakshuka Recipe: Quick and Delicious Breakfast Idea!

Whip up this simple and flavorful Shakshuka recipe for a quick and satisfying breakfast option.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Â¼ teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 (28-ounce can) whole peeled tomatoes
  • 6 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 5 sticks of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 5 sticks of fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add diced onion and bell pepper. Cook for about 5 minutes until the onion turns translucent.
  2. Add chopped garlic and spices. Cook for another minute.
  3. Pour the canned tomatoes and their juice into the pan. Use a spoon to break down the tomatoes. Season with salt and pepper, then bring the sauce to a simmer.
  4. Create small wells in the sauce with the back of your spoon and crack one egg into each well. Cook the eggs for 5 to 8 minutes until they reach your desired doneness. You can cover the pan with a lid to speed up the cooking process.
  5. Before serving, garnish with chopped cilantro and parsley.
